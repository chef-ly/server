Who is Chef-ly?

Chef-ly is a voice activate mobile app that will make your home cooking experience way more fun! Attempting a recipe for the first time? Don't fret, Chef-ly will guide you through a new recipe with ease. Just say "Hey Chef, what do I need to do next?" and Chef-ly won't leave you hanging.
